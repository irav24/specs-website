About the Conference

IEEE SPeCS 2027

The IEEE International Conference on Signal, Power and Computing Systems (SPeCS) is a premier international conference organized by the Department of Electrical Engineering, National Institute of Technology Silchar, with technical co-sponsorship from the IEEE Industry Applications Society Silchar Subsection Joint Chapter, IEEE Silchar Subsection, IEEE Signal Processing Society Kolkata Chapter, and IEEE Kolkata Section.

National Institute of Technology Silchar, established in 1967 as a Regional Engineering College and accorded the status of an Institute of National Importance in 2002, stands as one of the leading technical institutions in Assam, India. Nestled within a sprawling 600-acre lush green campus enriched with numerous natural lakes, the institute offers a vibrant academic environment.

Its commitment to innovation, interdisciplinary research, and academic excellence makes it an ideal host for an international conference of this stature. All accepted papers will appear in IEEE Xplore after successful registration.
